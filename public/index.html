Example Requests

Get production by ID:

?i=prod_1&apikey=yourkey

Search for "Hamlet":

?s=Hamlet&apikey=yourkey

Filter by city (London):

?s=London&city=London&apikey=yourkey

Productions starting in 2025:

?s=Hamlet&year=2025&apikey=yourkey
